All About Kyle

Introduction

My name is Kyle. I am currently a student at Columbia University's Business School Justice Through Code which is a program that teaches justice-impacted students how to code. I am currently in the first section of the program called Foundations which teaches students how to be job ready, prepare for interviews and the background checks, and how to write cover letters. Additionally, we are learning the foundations of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and will be finishing with an introduction to a few other languages. If successful in this first part, I will be ready to take the assessment and move on to the second section, Tech Pathways.
